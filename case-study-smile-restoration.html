Dental Case Study

Why Shree Sai Dental Clinic is the No.1 Choice for Smile Restorations

Home Blog Smile Restorations

A healthy smile not only improves appearance but also boosts confidence, speech, and overall oral health. At Shree Sai Dental Clinic, Boudh, we are committed to providing advanced dental solutions that help patients regain beautiful, confident smiles through personalized treatment and expert care by Dr. Bhagyaban Meher (Dental Surgeon).

Patient Background

A young patient visited our clinic with complaints of:

  • Irregularly aligned teeth.
  • Difficulty in biting and chewing.
  • Uneven smile appearance.
  • Lack of confidence while smiling and speaking.

The patient had been struggling with crowding and improper alignment of teeth for several years, affecting both appearance and oral hygiene.

Clinical Examination

After a detailed oral examination and dental assessment, Dr. Bhagyaban Meher identified:

  • Misaligned upper and lower teeth.
  • Crowding in the front tooth region.
  • Bite imbalance affecting smile aesthetics.
  • Need for orthodontic smile correction.

The patient was advised to undergo a customized smile restoration treatment plan.

Treatment Procedure

At Shree Sai Dental Clinic, every smile restoration is planned carefully according to the patient’s dental condition and facial aesthetics.

1

Detailed Smile Analysis

Digital photographs and clinical evaluations were performed to study tooth alignment, jaw position, and smile appearance.

2

Customized Orthodontic Treatment

A personalized treatment plan using modern orthodontic techniques was started to gradually align the teeth into their proper position.

3

Regular Monitoring

The patient attended regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress and ensure smooth alignment correction.

4

Smile Finishing & Polishing

After alignment correction, final smile adjustments and polishing procedures were completed to achieve a natural and attractive smile.

Results & Transformation

The results were highly successful:

  • Improved alignment of teeth.
  • Better bite function and chewing comfort.
  • Enhanced facial appearance and smile aesthetics.
  • Increased confidence in speaking and smiling.
  • Better oral hygiene maintenance.

The “before and after” transformation clearly showed a remarkable improvement in both dental health and smile appearance.

Conclusion

Smile restoration is not just about appearance — it is about restoring confidence, comfort, and long-term oral health. At Shree Sai Dental Clinic, we proudly help patients achieve healthier and more beautiful smiles with advanced dental expertise and compassionate care.
