About Our Clinic

Advanced Digital Dentistry in Boudh

Welcome to Shree Sai Dental Clinic, your destination for modern dental care located conveniently opposite Big V Mall. Led by Dr. Bhagyaban Meher (BDS, Dental Surgeon, Root Canal Specialist & Implantologist), our clinic embraces the latest in advanced digital dentistry to provide precise, comfortable, and faster treatments for patients of all ages.

We focus on a comprehensive range of services including 24/7 emergency care, gentle paediatric dentistry, bleeding gums treatment, and advanced dental implants. We also offer customized healthcare packages tailored to your needs and budget. Step into our clinic for a welcoming, pain-free experience designed for your healthy, happy smile.
